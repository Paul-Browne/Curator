Tooltips

A tooltip is usually a snippet of extra text that appears, similar in a way to the abbr element, but with style

<b class="hint--bottom hint--rounded" data-hint="when you hover over it">extra text that appears</b>

Never use a tooltip to reveal important information, since it is impossible to hover on mobile devices

Videos

To make video embeds fluid, Curator uses the jQuery plugin Fitvids

Simply add the class video to a container around the <iframe> and the plugin will make your video embeds resize responsively.


<div class="video tl-col-1-2">
    <iframe src="//www.youtube.com/embed/j2tE-BCwZtw?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
</div>
